Conclusion: GSG or GS configuration does seem to have some effect on impedance matching, but not that large. It is certainly not the reason left probe optimal pair_num is 160, while right probe optimal num is 100.¶

Summary¶

Data collected indicate that left probe and right probe has different optimal impedance match condition. We can:¶

1. choose pair_num to be ~135 such that both left and right probe can achieve -20dB¶

2. make left idt pair_num=160, right idt pair_num=100, so that they each achieve their own optimal impedance matching¶
